About Us

Founded in 1995, Germantown Shower Glass Company began with a vision to revolutionize bathroom design through premium glass solutions. What started as a small family business has grown into the region's most trusted name in custom shower enclosures, serving thousands of satisfied customers across the Mid-Atlantic region.

Our mission is to combine innovative design, superior materials, and expert craftsmanship to create stunning shower spaces that enhance the value and beauty of your home. We're committed to excellence in every project, backed by our core values of integrity, quality, and customer satisfaction.
